Our Story

Born from the Lowcountry

Holy City Catering was founded right here in Charleston with a simple belief: great food tastes better when it's connected to place. We partner with Lowcountry farmers, fishermen, and makers to build menus that tell the story of the South Carolina coast — from she-crab bisque to wood-smoked whole hog.

Whether you're planning a tented wedding reception on the marsh, a corporate luncheon in the historic district, or a backyard birthday feast for 30, our team handles every detail — from custom menu design to linen selection — so you can enjoy the moment you've been planning.

We are fully licensed, insured, and ServSafe certified. Our kitchen is located in Charleston and we serve Berkeley, Charleston, and Dorchester counties.
