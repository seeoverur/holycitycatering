Our Story

A Catering Company
Built on Good Food

For over a decade, Holy City Catering has been bringing people together around tables worth remembering — right here in the heart of the Lowcountry.

Who We Are

Rooted in the
Lowcountry

Holy City Catering was founded in Charleston, South Carolina with a straightforward idea: that food tastes better when it comes from somewhere real. The Lowcountry has one of the richest culinary traditions in the country — she-crab soup, stone-ground grits, fresh-caught shrimp pulled from local waters — and we built our entire approach around honoring that tradition.

Over the years we've had the privilege of feeding guests at weddings on the marsh, company dinners in the historic district, birthday celebrations on Sullivan's Island, and just about everything in between. Every event is different. Every menu is different. That variety is what keeps this work interesting and keeps our team passionate about what we do.

"Good food doesn't need a complicated story — it just needs honest ingredients and people who care."

We're a small, tight-knit operation that takes pride in the details. From the first planning conversation to the last plate cleared, we treat every event the way we'd want our own to be treated.

What We Stand For

The Things That
Matter to Us

Local First

We source from Lowcountry farms, fishermen, and makers whenever the season allows. Keeping it local means better flavor and a smaller footprint.

Quality Over Quantity

We take on a limited number of events so that each one gets the full attention it deserves. We'd rather do fewer things exceptionally well.

Genuine Hospitality

Southern hospitality isn't a marketing phrase for us — it's how we were raised. Every guest at every table is treated like family.

Professionalism

Fully licensed, insured, and ServSafe certified. We handle all the logistics so you don't have to think about anything except enjoying the event.

How We Work

Our Process

01
The Conversation

Every event starts with a simple conversation about your vision — the occasion, the guest count, the vibe, any dietary needs. No obligation, no pressure.

02
Menu Design

We build a custom menu around your event, the season, and your budget. We'll suggest pairings, flag what's fresh and local, and handle all the planning.

03
The Tasting

For larger events we offer tastings so you can experience the menu firsthand and make adjustments before the big day. Nothing gets finalized until you're happy.

04
Day-Of Execution

Our team arrives early, sets up completely, and handles every detail from first course to final cleanup. You show up and enjoy your own event.

A Sense of Place

Why Charleston

Charleston is one of the most food-rich cities in the American South. Centuries of culinary history — West African, English, French Huguenot, and Native American influences all layered together — have produced a regional cuisine unlike anywhere else in the country.

That heritage shapes everything we cook. Stone-ground grits from a mill that's been running for generations. White shrimp from local trawlers. Heirloom rice varieties that nearly disappeared and are now being revived by Lowcountry farmers. We don't just cook in Charleston — we cook from Charleston.

When guests sit down at one of our tables, we want them to taste a place as much as a meal.

Our Team

The People
Behind the Food

Holy City Catering is a small, dedicated team of cooks, servers, and event professionals who genuinely love what they do. Our culinary staff brings a range of backgrounds — from fine dining kitchens to family barbecue traditions — and that mix shows up on the plate in the best possible way.

We invest in our people. Everyone on staff is ServSafe certified. Our event leads have years of hands-on experience running events of every size. When you hire Holy City Catering, you're getting a team that shows up prepared, professional, and proud of the food they're serving.
